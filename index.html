madras Cream Center

Madras Cream Center is a cherished bakery located in the heart of Madras, known for its delectable array of freshly baked pastries, cakes, and cookies. Founded by the passionate baker Mr. Ravi Varma, it has been a beloved institution for decades. This quaint bakery has not only delighted taste buds but also created countless memories for the local community. Step inside Madras Cream Center for a slice of nostalgia and a taste of sweet perfection.
